Mortgage Calculator Guide: Everything You Need to Know

January 10, 2024 • 9 min read

In this guide: Learn how mortgage calculators work, understand PITI, and make informed home buying decisions.

What is a Mortgage Calculator?

A mortgage calculator estimates your monthly mortgage payment based on the home price, down payment, interest rate, and loan term. It's an essential tool for anyone shopping for a home or comparing refinance options.

Understanding PITI

Your monthly mortgage payment typically includes four components:

  • P - Principal: The amount that goes toward paying down your loan balance
  • I - Interest: The cost of borrowing money from your lender
  • T - Taxes: Property taxes collected by your local government
  • I - Insurance: Homeowners insurance to protect your property

Key Inputs Explained

  • Home Price: The purchase price of the home
  • Down Payment: The upfront cash you pay (typically 3-20%)
  • Interest Rate: The annual rate charged by your lender
  • Loan Term: How long you have to repay (15 or 30 years are most common)

Conclusion

Mortgage calculators are powerful tools for planning your home purchase. Use them early and often to understand what you can afford and how different factors affect your payment.
